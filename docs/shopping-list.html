BIOQUA

AI-Assisted Water Quality Monitoring System

Project by: Agreda, G.M., Joven, C.L., Mendez, A.V., Tangao, W., Zamora, G.D. | 2026

What is BIOQUA?

BIOQUA is an automated water quality monitoring system that detects bacteria in water samples using Arduino sensors, automated Gram staining, digital microscopy, and AI image classification. It outputs a water quality risk level (Low / Moderate / High) on an LCD display.

Safety Rules

  • Nitrile gloves + eye protection at all times
  • Crystal violet permanently stains everything — cover all work surfaces
  • Ethanol/acetone (decolorizer) is flammable — no open flames nearby
  • Dispose slides and reagent waste in 10% bleach soak for 30 minutes
  • Only use safe water sources (tap, pond, soil water) — no sewage
  • Adult supervision required for staining and soldering
